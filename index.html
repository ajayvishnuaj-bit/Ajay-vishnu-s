00 — About

The frame before the frame.

I'm a Senior CG/VFX Layout Artist with seven years spent turning storyboards and previs into the cinematic camera work that everything downstream — animation, FX, lighting, comp — builds on. My job starts before a shot looks finished: reading a director's intent, finding the lens, the scale, the blocking that tells the story clearly, and holding that continuity across sequences with hundreds of shots and tight deadlines.


At DNEG I've carried plate-based and full-CG layout across action-heavy theatrical releases, working shot handoffs with matchmove, animation, FX, lighting and compositing teams, and absorbing feedback cycles without losing delivery quality. Recognized as Best Artist of the Month for consistency and reliability under pressure.
